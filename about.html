What is Agent-Based Modelling?

Agent Based Modelling is a computational modelling approach that simulates the actions and interactions of autonomous agents to assess their effects on the system as a whole.

What Epidemix Does
Epidemix leverages Agent-Based Modeling (ABM) to evaluate the impact of a vaccine on the transmission dynamics of meningococcal meningitis. How the whole system works is used to inform and shape public health strategies and optimize vaccination campaigns to control and ultimately eradicate meningitis within a target timeframe.
Key Benefits
  1. Individual-level simulation
  2. Understand Complex system dynamics
  3. Flexiblity and scenario training
  4. Spatial and Temporal Resolution
  5. Policy simualation
  6. Adaptability to diffent data sets
  7. Behavioral insights
  8. Visualization
